Places to go!

Cartagena

One of te best preserved colonial cities in the americas is Cartagena, the jewel of Colombia's Caribbean coast. Getsemani, a laid-back neighborhood beyong the historic city center, and BocaGrande essentially a bran-new neighborhood, are located along the oceanfront. Islands and beaches are also just a boat ride away, making them perfect destinations for day outings and retreats.

Medellin

One of colombias famous cities, Medellin is known for being the "City of Eternal Spring. It is located in a bowl in the andes mountains. So wherever you go, you'll be able to catch a glimpse of mountains in Medellin. Lots of iguanas roam the city such as iguanas, and even monkeys! It is home to one of Colombia's most picturesque departments. Antioquia is home to stunning natural scenery, charming small villages, coffee estates, and even the Caribbean coast! .

El Peñón de Guatapé

650 stairs ascend up the steep slope of the spetacular "La Piedra Del Penol, or 'The Big Guatape Rock', through a narrow crevice. The view from the summit of Guatape rock is incredibly beautiful, and it has to be one of Colombia's most amazing lookouts. At 2,135 meters above sea level, the rock provides a natrual beauty and the ideal position to take in panoramic views of Guatape Lake. .
